Police Training in Pittsburgh

By: Noah Hubbard

In 2013, following acquittal of George Zimmerman for the death of Trayvon Martin, a movement called Black Lives Matter was born. By 2014, issues of police violence, and other systemic inequalities related to the justice system in the United States became apart of the national discussion in ways not seen before. In this same time period, the Pittsburgh Police Department saw major changes in how they spent their time training officers.
